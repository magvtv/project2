Awesome Ghanaians Names


More of a tradition than a normal custom, the Ghanaian people cherish the way of naming children during birth. Here, you are going to know your own Akan name by just the day you were born! It is almost certain that it tells more about your own character...

The tradition goes way back to a tribe in West Africa called the Akan tribe.The names have a deeper meaning which will quite intrigue you to know more about it. Signifigantly, there are also prominent personalities with such names as their titles; one of them (just to mention) being the UN Secretary General Kofi Annan!
